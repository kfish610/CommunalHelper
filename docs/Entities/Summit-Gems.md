*   **Summit Gems** and **Summit Gem Manager** - **Summit Gems** that you can actually use for your map/campaign. The **Summit Gem Manager** entity is placed in the room where it checks if all the specified gems are collected, and moves the Crystal Heart in the room to its own position, with an optional offset. Two additional custom gem texture/sound options are included, Also works with [Adventure Helper](https://gamebanana.com/gamefiles/9807) Custom Hearts, and [CollabUtils](https://gamebanana.com/gamefiles/12784) MiniHearts.
